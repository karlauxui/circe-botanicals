About Us

According to Greek legend, the goddess Circe used her deep knowledge of herbs and botanicals to conjure magic and make transformative potions. At Circe Botanicals, we believe nature knows best–it can nurture, heal, and sustain. Our products address the needs of all skin types, while also honoring the earth with their all-natural formulations.

All of Circe’s products are:

  • Leaping Bunny certified, 100% vegan and non-GMO
  • Sustainably sourced with 100% plant-derived ingredients
  • Naturally pigmented (no FD&C heavy metal dyes or colorants)
  • Packaged in biodegradable, non-petroleum based materials.
